iPhone 16 Pro Max

​The iPhone 16 Pro Max, introduced by Apple in September 2024, is the company's largest and most advanced smartphone to date. It features a massive 6.9-inch display, offering an immersive viewing experience. Under the hood, it is powered by Apple's A18 Pro chip, built on a 3-nanometer process, ensuring top-tier performance and efficiency. The device boasts a triple-camera setup: a 48 MP primary wide-angle lens, a 48 MP ultra-wide-angle lens, and a 12 MP telephoto lens with 5x optical zoom, enabling versatile photography options. Additionally, it supports 4K video recording at up to 120fps.
